About Bibles for America

What is Bibles for America?

Bibles for America (BfA) is a nonprofit organization whose mission is to distribute free copies of the New Testament Recovery Version and Christian literature throughout the United States.

What are your beliefs?

BfA was established in 2000 as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization with the mission to distribute free copies of the New Testament Recovery Version and Christian literature throughout the U.S. Its staff members are all born-again Christians. Please see our statement of faith to read about our beliefs.

How is Bibles for America supported financially?

Bibles for America (BfA) is funded solely by contributions, which are tax-deductible according to Internal Revenue Code section 501(c)(3). An independent Board of Directors governs major financial decisions. All contributions are appreciated and used solely for spreading the Word of God.

About ordering free Bibles and books

How many Bibles and books can I order?

In order to give away free Bibles and books in as widespread a way as possible, we have set a limit of one free Bible and one copy of each book per person. To purchase additional Bibles and books, please contact the publisher, Living Stream Ministry.

Do you ship materials outside the United States?

Bibles for America’s free Bible and free Christian books are only offered in the United States; we do not ship to other countries. However, depending on what country you live in, one of our affiliates or Rhema Inc., an international Christian literature distributor, may be able to ship free literature to you. For military service members, we can ship to APO addresses in Iraq, Afghanistan, and other foreign countries. Military service members and their families can order our free materials at www.bfa.org/military.

Do you ship to prison inmates?

Bibles for America does not supply materials to prison chaplains or inmates.

How long does it take for me to get my free Bible and free Christian books in the mail?

From the time the order is placed online, please allow up to 4 weeks for delivery.

Contribution

How can I give to Bibles for America (BfA)?

You may give to BfA in three ways:

Online—Make a gift online using a credit card.
Mail—Send a check or money order to Bibles for America, 355 Goddard, Suite 100, Irvine, CA 92618.
Phone—Call 888.551.0102, ext. 314 to make a gift by credit card.

For other types of contributions, please read Ways to Contribute.

Are contributions to BfA tax deductible?

Yes, Bibles for America (BfA) is a nonprofit organization funded solely by contributions, which are tax-deductible according to Internal Revenue Code section 501(c)(3). If you or your financial advisor would like a copy of our 501(c)(3) letter from the Internal Revenue Service, please call us at 888.551.0102, ext. 314.

Does BfA share my personal information with other organizations or businesses?

No, BfA does not disclose your personal information for commercial purposes or share it with any other organizations. Read our Privacy Policy.

Does BfA send receipts for online contributions?

Yes, when you make an online gift, BfA sends you an acknowledgment in an automated e-mail message within minutes after you submit your contribution. BfA will also mail a personalized letter acknowledging your gift the following month. For your convenience, in January we will send you a letter listing all the gifts you made during the previous calendar year.

Is it possible to make a memorial gift to BfA?

Yes, a gift to BfA can be made in memory of an individual. We will send you a letter of acknowledgment, and a letter will be sent to the family of the deceased to notify them of your memorial gift. When you make your gift online, a space is provided for you to enter contact information of those you would like us to inform of your memorial gift.

How can I edit or cancel my recurring gift?

Anytime you make either a one-time or recurring gift, BfA automatically sends you an acknowledgment e-mail. In that e-mail you will be provided with a link and a password to access your giving account online. Thereafter, you can edit or cancel your recurring gift online at any time by simply using your username (your e-mail address) and password to log on at My Account and selecting Manage Recurring Gifts from the sidebar. From this page you can change the amount of your gift, cancel your gift, update your credit card information, or change the day of the month you would like to have your credit card charged. If you prefer, you may call us at 888.551.0102, ext. 314 to edit or cancel your recurring gift.

How can I update my personal information?

Anytime you make either a one-time or recurring gift, BfA automatically sends you an acknowledgment e-mail. In that e-mail you will be provided with a link and a password to access your giving account online. Thereafter, you can update your personal information by using your username (your e-mail address) and password to log on at My Account and select Update Personal Information from the sidebar. If your address has changed, please check to see whether the billing address on your credit card also needs updating.

How can I view my giving history online?

When you log on at My Account (see above two questions), your giving history will immediately appear.

Can I give to BfA without setting up an online account?

It is not necessary to set up an online account in order to give to BfA. BfA automatically creates an online account for you when you make a gift online. We offer you the option of accessing your account as a convenience to you so that you can quickly and easily view your giving history.

Does BfA accept other types of gifts such as stocks and securities, gift annuities, or real estate?

Yes, BfA is able to accept noncash gifts. You can find out about the various types of giving by reading Ways to Contribute.

Does BfA accept wire transfers?

Yes, BfA accepts wire transfers. Please call 888.551.0102, ext. 310 to obtain wiring instructions.
